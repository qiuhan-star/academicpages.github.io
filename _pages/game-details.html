Take a leap into the academic adventure with the PhD Simulator!

About the Game:

The PhD Simulator is a unique game crafted by a PhD graduate from the University of Washington, currently employed at Google. This game was developed to celebrate the author's successful graduation and mirrors the many challenges of a PhD journey within its gameplay.

Gameplay:

The game is straightforward, featuring scenarios and choices that reflect the academic experience. As you make decisions, the 'Hope' meter at the top left corner of the screen changes.

My Experience:

I found the game to be a realistic reflection of the PhD journey. It taught me the importance of balancing work and rest, maintaining focus, setting clear goals, and acting on ideas promptly.

What I've Gained from Gaming

🔵 Balance Work and Rest: Taking a break is essential to continue with research. 🛌 Rest is key to going the distance!

🌟 Focus! Maintaining concentration is crucial for success. 🤓 Stay focused and hit the target!

📚 Avoid Being a Slacker, Set Clear Goals: Establish clear objectives and don't just go with the flow. 🏆 Having goals gives you direction!

💡 Act on Your Ideas: Once you have an idea, act on it immediately without hesitation. Good topics are limited; if you can think of it, so can others. ⏩ Seize the inspiration and act fast!

Who Should Play

For those considering a PhD, playing the game multiple times can provide insights into the challenges involved. Current PhD students without a clear direction may find valuable guidance on how to graduate on time. Professors looking to give their students a realistic glimpse into the PhD journey should consider incorporating the game into their curriculum.

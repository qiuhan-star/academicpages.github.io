PhD Simulator Game Details

Take a leap into the academic adventure with the PhD Simulator!

About the Game: The PhD Simulator is a unique game crafted by a PhD graduate from the University of Washington, currently employed at Google. This game was developed to celebrate the author's successful graduation and mirrors the many challenges of a PhD journey within its gameplay.

Gameplay: The game is straightforward, featuring scenarios and choices that reflect the academic experience. As you make decisions, the 'Hope' meter at the top left corner of the screen changes.

My Experience: I found the game to be a realistic reflection of the PhD journey. It taught me the importance of balancing work and rest, maintaining focus, setting clear goals, and acting on ideas promptly.

Takeaways:

  • Balance work and rest: Taking breaks is essential for sustained research productivity.
  • Focus: Stay concentrated on your goals.
  • Clarity: Be clear about your objectives and don't be a passive participant.
  • Initiative: Act on your ideas without hesitation; if you think of a good research topic, others might too.

For those considering a PhD, I recommend playing the game multiple times to understand the challenges involved. For current PhD students without a clear direction, it might provide insights on how to graduate on time. For professors who wish to give their students a glimpse into the realities of a PhD journey, I highly recommend introducing them to the PhD Simulator game.
